Personal Transformation Sessions

-- Punch Line -- On your journey to the summit of a soul-inspired life, master guides can help you navigate the path.

Changing the world means exercising authenticity, integrity and compassion, raising awareness and taking positive action as appropriate. This should be a wholly inspiring and creative process, yet before long the cumulative effects of stress can take their toll, and habits of over-work and negative thinking begin to set in.

  • How to keep positive in the face of the negative experiences and emotions of your clients?
  • How to achieve life balance when there never seems to be a natural end to the working day?
  • How to maintain passion, motivation and creativity within a large bureaucratic organisation?

Our Approach

Our unique, integrated approach combines top-class coaching with groundbreaking energetic alignment training to help you discover your own solutions to these and other challenges. You will learn powerful ways to manage stress, clear negativity and find fulifilment at both personal and professional levels.

The process is transformative as it provides direction and clarity in an uncertain world where it feels like we have to paddle ever faster just to stay afloat. As working hours stretch, time for reflection seems to shrink, thinking becomes clouded and our goals, dreams and desires are obscured. Before long we feel as if we are facing upstream.

The solution lies in building in regular sessions for creative thinking and reflection which provide the basis for making clear choices and taking concrete action. Alongside our unique energetic alignment training, this will always lead you downstream as you begin to enter the natural flow of your life.

The result is success, however you choose to define it. And since all sustainable change starts from the inside out, you may even be inspired to discover new ways to meet your values and achieve your goals!

Our Programs

Clients may book a series of 4,8 or 12 personal transformation sessions according to need and preference. Each session lasts 60 minutes, and is conducted face-to-face or over Skype. With a good Internet connection, clients may therefore benefit from our programs wherever they are based in the world.


Please contact us directly for free initial consultation or further information about our programs.
