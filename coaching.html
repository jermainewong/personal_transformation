The Challenge of Change

Changing the world means exercising integrity and compassion, raising awareness of relevant issues, and taking positive action as appropriate.

This should be an inspiring and creative process, yet before long the effects of stress can take their toll, and habits of over-work or negative thinking begin to set in.

  • How to keep positive in the face of the negative experiences and emotions of your clients?
  • How to achieve life balance when there never seems to be a natural end to the working day?
  • How to maintain passion, motivation and creativity within a large bureaucratic organisation?

How can coaching help?

Coaching is a professional, collaborative process which helps you to access your inspiration and maximize your potential.

Through a process of active listening, open questioning and ongoing support, the coach acts as a catalyst for you to clarify your values and desires, and achieve positive results in any area of your life, including career, relationships, health and personal development.

When you start 'being', you operate from your soul. When you start 'doing', your mind took over.

Our Approach

Our unique, integrated approach combines top-class coaching with groundbreaking energy alignment techniques to help you discover your own solutions to your challenges.

You will learn powerful ways to release stress, clear negativity and find fulifilment at both personal and professional levels.

How are we different?

Traditional coaching places emphasis on goals and actions. Any personal challenges, including fears or limiting beliefs, are addressed along the way.

While this may be helpful, these challenges are often not dealt with effectively and they may re-emerge, even after the coaching relationship has ended.

A horizontal, goal-based focus may also reinforce a sense of 'delayed gratification' where clients fail to find fulfilment in the present moment - the only time we really have.

At PTGC we focus on the vertical as well as the horizontal - the art and science of 'being' as the essential foundation for the 'doing'.

Clients find it easier to identify and achieve their aims as a natural consequence of this state. And the step-by-step nature of the process becomes just as enjoyable as the end result.

What are the Benefits?

Our coaching is transformative as it provides direction and clarity in an uncertain world where it feels like we have to paddle ever faster just to stay afloat.

As working hours stretch, time for reflection seems to shrink, thinking becomes clouded and our dreams are obscured. Before long we feel as if we are facing upstream.

The solution lies in building in regular sessions for creative thinking and reflection which provide the basis for making clear choices and taking concrete action.

Alongside our powerful energy alignment training, you will move effortlessly towards your goals by engaging fully in the present nature of the creation process. This will always lead you downstream as you enter the natural flow of your life.

You will soon begin to feel a sense of fulfillment and success, however you choose to define it. And since all sustainable change starts from the inside out, you may even be inspired to discover new ways to meet your values and achieve your goals!

Our Programs

Personal Coaching

Clients may book a series of 4,8 or 12 personal coaching sessions according to need and preference. Each session lasts 60 minutes, and is conducted face-to-face or over Skype. With a reliable Internet connection, clients may benefit from our programs wherever they are based in the world.

Please contact us directly for a free initial consultation.

Organizational Coaching

We also facilitate group and team coaching sessions in the workplace, helping managers and co-workers identify and achieve their internal and external goals. Our programs focus on three key areas: stress-free performance, effective communication and alignment.

Coaching sessions may be held in the workplace or at an external location, including workshops or retreats at our principle locations of Pai in northern Thailand and Ibiza, Spain.

We would be happy to present our services to your company or organization upon request.
