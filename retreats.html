Retreats at Pai

Come and enjoy a relaxing holiday in the mountains of Northern Thailand and achieve a break-through in your life at the same time!

During your stay in the beautiful town of Pai, near Chiang Mai, you can take advantage of personal coaching and energy alignment sessions with Mark Alliison, the founder of PTGC.

You may also choose to book follow-up coaching sessions via Skype when you return home.

We are happy to help you arrange your travel and accommodation and advise on the other benefits that Pai has to offer.

During your visit, we offer you a full package of coaching and energy alignment sessions depending on the length of your stay (usually 4-12 sessions).

These will be conducted for 90 minutes each morning. The afternoons and evenings will be free for you to enjoy the delights of Pai and the surrounding countryside.

In addition to the sessions, we would be pleased to arrange a variety of relaxing activities at your request, including:

  • trekking and bamboo rafting
  • elephant rides
  • Thai cooking course
  • Thai and oil massage
  • Visits to local temples, waterfalls, caves and other attractions
  • Hot spring bathing

We will help you keep costs to a minimum by charging in line with local prices. We believe this is a great-value alternative to other coaching services offered at international rates.

And you will have the time and space to focus on your life, and take a holiday at the same time!

The Peace of Pai

Pai sits on the banks of a river and is surrounded by stunning natural beauty. It is home to several of Thailand's hilltribe communities and attracts a mix of foreign and Thai visitors throughout the year.

Pai has a wide variety of accommodation to suit all tastes and budgets. The town also has a great range of restaurants serving high quality Thai and international cuisine at some of the cheapest prices in Thailand. Small local bars provide an easy-going nightlife - many with live music.

For the adventurous, a variety of activities are available, such as white-water rafting, jungle trekking and elephant rides. But with its waterfalls, hot springs and rice fields, Pai is also the perfect place to relax and enjoy 'being' rather than 'doing'!
