At Personal Transformation for Global Change (PTGC) we understand the challenges working for change in a world that often seems to be moving in the opposite direction.

Through our transformational programs you will discover how to:

Release stress and negativity
  • Transform any limiting beliefs that are holding you back
  • Release emotional blockages from your physical body and energy field
  • Use simple mindfulness and visualisation techniques to maintain a positive mindset
Tap into your intuition and inspiration
  • Connect to the intuitive power of your heart as a guiding force in life
  • Develop better heart-brain communication and coherence
  • Identify and pursue your authentic aims and desires in life

  • Think outside the box and experience more light-bulb moments

About Mark

I created PTGC because I am passionate about the power of human potential to create positive change. As a cerfified coach and energetic healing practitioner, my passion is to help individuals around the world discover their passion and share it with others in positive, inspiring and creative ways.

With my professional background in human rights research and advocacy, I enjoy working with those who feel drawn to make a difference in the world. My clients come from all walks of life, but many work for social change in governmental, charity or humanitarian organizations. While it may match their values, this work often generates levels of stress which compromise balance, well-being and enjoyment of life.
