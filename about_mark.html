About Mark

My journey...

As a child, I never really knew what I wanted to be when I grew up. All I knew was that I wanted to live in different countries. It was that clear, not just to travel but to live for a while in different places around the world. The only thing I really loved in school was learning languages, so I guess that set me up well!

It only recently occurred to me that I have fulfilled that dream, although I really don't feel I did much consciously to make it happen. Somehow the choices and opporunities just arose and I took advantage of them.

As a Coach, I've come to realise that this kind of passage through life is natural - making choices based on opportunities that emerge. If you are following your heart, doing what feels right for you at the time, you can never make the 'wrong' choice.

Sure, things may turn out differently from how you expect, but you will always be able to make new choices later on based on your learning. And when we start to see this process as a string of choices and learning rather than make-or-break decisions, we can relax into the moment and go with the flow.
